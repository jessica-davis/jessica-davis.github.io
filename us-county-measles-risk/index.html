Risk Estimates for Measles Outbreaks

Select a tab to view different types of risk estimates for measles for U.S. counties. Each map is interactive and shows county-level data with hover details.

  • Importation Risk: measures the likelihood of disease cases being introduced from external sources such as travel or trade
  • Recent Vaccination Coverage: MMR coverage in children (specific definitions varies by state) taken from "Trends in County-Level MMR Vaccination Coverage in Children in the United States" by Dong et al.
  • Outbreak Risk: assesses the overall outbreak risk combining the importation risk with the vaccination coverage of children
  • Epidemiological Data:Surveillance data of the last 2 weeks

Gray areas indicate counties with no available data.
